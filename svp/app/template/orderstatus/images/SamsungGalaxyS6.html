Meet the reimagined Samsung Galaxy S6

Reinvented from the outside in

Finally, a smartphone that's more than simply beautiful. The Samsung Galaxy S® 6 has evolved its design—both inside and out. The Samsung Galaxy S6 lets you:

  • Stay connected to everything all on the incredibly slim and lightweight smartphone.
  • Watch images and videos come to life on the 5.1" Quad HD Super AMOLED® display, even in sunlight.
  • Work, watch, and game at blazing-fast speeds.
  • Capture amazing photos with the fast-focus camera and take epic wide-angle selfies.
  • Use Ultra Power Saving Mode,1 Fast Charging,2 or wireless charging3 to extend your smartphone's battery life when it's running low.

DESIGN

Compact meets stylish

Stay entertained and connected while on the go with this easy-to-hold smartphone. The Galaxy S6 combines a stunning all-metal bezel and sophisticated glass to create an incredibly slim and lightweight smartphone. It's the perfect size to take with you virtually anywhere!

DISPLAY

A screen like you've never seen

Books, games, photos, and more come to life on the incredibly colorful 5.1" Quad HD Super AMOLED display. Whether you're reading, surfing the Web, or watching a show, experience high-resolution images and vibrant color—even in sunlight.

16MP CAMERA

Picture perfect

Capture every moment beautifully with premium camera features you'll never believe came from a smartphone. Easily shoot 16MP rear-facing and 5MP front-facing photos with just the double tap of the home button. The wide-angle lens on the front-facing camera is perfect for taking epic selfies! Plus, with auto High Dynamic Range (HDR) you can capture clear shots in almost any light.

BATTERY CHARGING

The power of three

Spend more time using your Galaxy S6 and less time charging. Tap Ultra Power Saving Mode at 10% charge and still get up to 24 hours of talk and text time.1 Use Fast Charging to power up to 50% in about 30 minutes.2 Or cut the cord and recharge wirelessly.3
